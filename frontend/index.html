Welcome to Collab Hub

Collab Hub is a platform where faculties of different colleges can post events, and students can register for those events, collaborate, and get mentored. Join us and take part in exciting hackathons and more!

Faculty

Faculties can post events organized by their college or university. They can also mentor teams, guide them, and give suggestions through a dedicated Q&A section.

Students

Students can view the calendar of upcoming events, find interesting hackathons, register for them, and collaborate with teams.
